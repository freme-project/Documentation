Getting started with e-Terminology

Introduction

Use e-Terminology for term anotation within the text. It can be used for detecting terms and enriching them with term related information.

Service provides access to the Tilde terminology services, providing terms from industry lead terminology databases such as EuroTermBank, Microsoft Language Portal, InterActive Terminology for Europe (IATE). Also provides posssibility of enrichment with external terminology databases. Currently supports of terminology annotation with external resource AgroVoc. Other external databases on demand, contact Tilde team via email langserv@tilde.com

Supports of 26 languages via the Tilde Terminology service.

Step 1 - annotate with e-Terminology

Annotates terms within the source text

HTML Request

POST /current/e-terminology/tilde?source-lang=en&target-lang=de HTTP/1.1
Host: api.freme-project.eu
Accept: text/turtle
Content-Type: text/plain
   
Language is the ability to acquire complex systems of communication.

Curl

curl -X POST -H "Accept: text/turtle" -H "Content-Type: text/plain" -d 'Language is the ability to acquire and use complex systems of communication. ' "http://api.freme-project.eu/current/e-terminology/tilde?source-lang=en&target-lang=de"

Step 2 - Understanding the output of e-Terminology

The result of e-Terminology can be diveded in 2 parts:

  1. Term anotations with references to terminology entries at term.tilde.com platform. Anotations done, using NIF ontology including ITS 2.0 data categories.
  2. Terminology data, terminology concepts with full information about terms, converted from TBX to OntoLex Lemon format.

OntoLex (Lemon)

Classical Lemon model contains Lexicon for each language with Lexical entries, Lexical senses and Lexical forms. But terminology resources mostly are concept based and organoized in collections, therefore e-Terminology data model extends calssical Lemon model with concepts, its definition and treir mappings to collection and domain classification system.

Anotated object in NIF are referenceing to concept (SKOS:Concept object) with a reference itsrdf:termInfoRef.
