How to translate from English to Dutch with e-Translation and e-Terminology

Introduction

Use e-Terminology before e-Translation to improve or adjust translation senses. e-Terminology identifies terms within the source text and help machine translation use them. You can introduce your own terminology collection to forse MT using your own terminology from term.tilde.com portal.

Technical Instructions

Step 1 - using e-Terminology

Annotate terms within the source text

Curl

-X POST -d 'The EU in brief. The EU is a unique economic and political partnership between 28 European countries that together cover much of the continent.' 'http://api-dev.freme-project.eu/current/e-terminology/tilde?informat=text&outformat=json-ld&source-lang=en&target-lang=nl'

Result

There are following terms found (term rank in brackets): continent [1], partnership [0.8961], briefing [0.5946], economics [0.3165], covering [0]

Step 2: using e-Translation to translate

Translate text taking in account annotated term in Step 1.

The input data is the output of Step 1 - response of e-Terminology
